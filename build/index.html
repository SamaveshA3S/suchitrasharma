ABOUT

Sociologist | Author | Professor
M.A. (Gold Medalist), M.Phil., Ph.D., M.Sc. (Value Education & Spirituality)

Dr. Suchitra Sharma is a distinguished retired Professor of Sociology at Govt. V.Y.T.PG Autonomous College, Durg (C.G.), and currently serves as a Visiting Professor at Bharti University, Durg. With over four decades of teaching experience, Dr. Sharma has established herself as a leading authority in sociology, value education, and spirituality.

Her expertise spans across various domains of social research, with a particular focus on transgender community studies since 2018. Dr. Sharma's interdisciplinary approach combines sociological theory with practical applications in spiritual counselling and health, making her a unique voice in contemporary academic discourse.

As both a social and spiritual counselor, Dr. Sharma bridges the gap between academic research and community service. Her extensive publication record, including 60 research papers in national and international journals, reflects her commitment to advancing sociological knowledge and understanding.

Research & Current Work

Dr. Sharma's current research focuses on the Transgender Community, a project she has been passionately pursuing since 2018. This groundbreaking work addresses critical social issues and contributes to the broader understanding of gender identity and social inclusion in contemporary society.

Her research methodology combines traditional sociological approaches with innovative spiritual counselling techniques, creating a holistic framework for understanding complex social phenomena.
